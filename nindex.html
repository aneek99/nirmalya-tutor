About

The story behind the teaching

Teaching has been a part of my life since my college days. While pursuing my B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering, I started helping classmates and discovered that explaining things clearly was something I genuinely loved.

After graduation, I spent nine years building a professional career — including more than two years in the EdTech industry — while continuing to mentor students on the side. In 2022, I made the decision to teach full-time.

Today, I work with students from Classes 6 to 10 in Mathematics and Physics. My approach is straightforward: build the foundations right, make concepts genuinely clear, and results follow.
